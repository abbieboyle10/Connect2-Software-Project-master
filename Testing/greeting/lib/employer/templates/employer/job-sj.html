Ideal Personality type

Beaver

The ideal personality type for this job is Beaver. For more infomration on the different animal types click here.

Organised

Beavers information-gathering process is practical and realistic to which they prefer to give organisation and structure. At work, Beavers make phenomenal administrators of systems which require precision. They tend to have incredibly high standards and seek predictability, stability and comfort. At work, they tend to prefer environments with no surprises and where rules and regulations are established and adhered to.

Team Players

Beavers are loyal, patient and gentle. They are often the glue that holds a team together as they seek to nurture and support others. They are incredibly compassionate, caring and they enjoy looking after others to ensure their safety. Beavers are happy to be part of a team, whether it is a follower or a leader. They have a desire to protect their community and values.

Practical

For a start, all beavers share a ‘sensing’ preference. This means they tend to focus on facts and details, rather than ideas and concepts. Due to this, all beavers tend not to be attracted to idle speculations and are most at home in the real world. At work, they have an eye toward practical applications in managing material things.

Proactive

Beavers are informative and reactive. They tend to work to spot problems before they occur and act in advance. When they communicate with others, they tend to do direct them. They are highly motivated to use their organizational skills and follow procedures as for Beavers in the long run loyalty, discipline, and teamwork get the job done right.

Dutiful

Beavers take pride in being dependable and trustworthy; if there's a job to be done, they can be counted on to put their shoulder to the wheel. Beavers also believe in law and order, and sometimes worry that respect for authority, even a fundamental sense of right and wrong, is being lost. Perhaps this is why Beavers honor customs and traditions so strongly -- they are familiar patterns that help bring stability to our modern, fast-paced world.

Cooperative

Beavers are the glue of civilization, maintaining and nurturing institutions that have been established by the dint of hard work. They tend to be conventional and cooperative in their work, wanting to make sure everybody gets what they deserve, no more and no less. They follow the rules of their cohort or group and expect others to as well. A Beavers self-esteem is based on their dependability; their self-respect on their beneficence; and their self-confidence on their respectability.
