    The Services We Offer

    Employer Profile

    Employers have the opportunity to create profiles for their company. Here they can list the relevant information for potential future employees to view.

    Employee Profile

    Employees can use Connect2 to create professional profiles based around their skills and personality. Employees can add their skills and experiences to their Connect2 profile.

    Complete Personality Testing

    Employee users can use Connect2 to understand more about their behaviour in the working world. Connect2 provides its own personality testing for employee users to complete.

    Apply to Jobs Anonymously

    Employee users can use Connect2 to apply to job listings directly. When employee users apply, their professional profile is submitted to the employer and kept anonymous.

    Manage Applications

    Connect2 allows Employers to manage their applications. Connect2 scores each candidate using its own ATS system and allows Employers to view and select candidates they are interested in.

    Schedule Interviews

    Connect2 offers an interview scheduling service to Employer users. This can be used to arrange interviews with applicants they are interested in. When an interview is scheudle, Employee users have the option to share their details.
