Ideal Personality type

Fox

The ideal personality type for this job is Fox. For more infomration on the different animal types click here.

Action Oriented

Foxes are the most action oriented folk of them all. They absorb information through sensation; what they can see, smell, touch, taste, and hear. They are extremely aware of their outer environments and how to adapt to them. They think quickly on their feet and know how to make the best of any moment, no matter how gloomy it may seem.

Adept problem-solving skills

Due to having an innate ability to stay in the moment, Foxes are natural problem solvers, finding solutions to crises quickly. Foxes have a special sense of immediate needs and can use their resourcefulness to find solutions. At work, Foxes are geniuses to have around when crises errupt and are naturally attracted to careers which have immediate, tangible rewards.

Opportunist

Foxes love adventure and will not miss an opportunity that may prove to be thrilling, pleasing or otherwise valuable. Driven by curosity, they are willing to try new methods to solving a problem, making them huge opportunists. Foxes tend to get started early on new opportunities because the doing is much more important than consideration beforehand.

Creative

Foxes are bursting with creativity. In general, they like to work with their hands because of their sensing preference meaning they are skilled at turning creative ideas into reality. Foxes tend to wind up in technical and creative fields as they provide outlets for their creativity and curiosity about how the world works.

Adaptability

Foxes live in the present moment and prefer to take a day at work as it comes so an unexpected hitch in a project or task is very unlikely to throw them off. In fact, these are the people you want around for emergencies, as they thrive on finding practical, concrete solutions for unexpected problems. New piece of technology in the workplace? equipment malfunction? Time to re-brand? you know who to call.

Detail Oriented

Foxes tend to focus on facts and details, rather than ideas and concepts due to their sensing preference. Foxes data collection is practical and realistic. They make excellent negotiators and troubleshooters through having a strong grounding in the reality of the moment. They are naturally talented at focusing on the details of present tasks and projects.
